A custom wiki on the Roblox game Road to Gramby's (RTG/rtg) by Mumble Games. This website (which is still wip) includes item information, blog posts and other relevant content. Everything on this website was made with blood, sweat and tears. For those of you needing a quick painting tool for rtg, I'll save you the trouble and tell you it's the paintbrush icon on the left (fourth one down).


The Road

What little is known of the game's lore is that, on the planet Papatopia (which is shared with other mumble games lore), you are tasked to find your gramby [possibly grandma]. The landscape of your region of Papatopia becomes increasingly more hostile to your journey as you go farther from your garage.
